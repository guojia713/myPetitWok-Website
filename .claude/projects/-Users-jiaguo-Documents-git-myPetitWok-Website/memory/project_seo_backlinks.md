When the app goes live on App Store and Google Play, submit to Product Hunt on launch day for maximum visibility. Also submit to AlternativeTo, AppAdvice, and food communities (Reddit r/asiancooking, r/chinesefood, r/MealPrepSunday).

**Why:** Backlinks are the biggest lever for ranking higher when people search "Asian recipes app" or "application mobile recettes asiatiques". Deferred because the app is not yet on the stores.

**How to apply:** Remind the user to do this when they mention the app store launch. Suggest Product Hunt as the first priority.
